Submitting your documents

You can send your documents to us online, by mail, or by fax.

Online

Submit your documents through:

  • My Account for Individuals
  • Represent a Client
  • My Business Account

By mail or fax

Send your documents to the Centre of Expertise for your region.

What to include on your cover page

To help us route your information correctly, please include a cover page with:

  • the name of your dispositions officer
  • your Centre of Expertise
  • your case, file, or tax identification number
